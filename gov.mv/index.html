Our Four Islands

我们的四座岛屿

Each carries its own story — together they form the soul of Mariven.

每一座岛都有自己的故事——合在一起就是马里文的灵魂。

Makadi

Main Island · 910,000 people

主岛 · 91万人口

The political and economic heart — Katora City, the western sugarcane plains, the Tapu volcano, and three millennia of history beneath every footstep.

政治与经济的心脏——卡托拉市、西部蔗田平原、塔普火山，以及脚下三千年的历史。

📍 910,000人 · 11,200 km²
🏛️ 首府：卡托拉市
🌋 塔普火山

Timo

Kava Island · 145,000 people

卡瓦之岛 · 14.5万人口

Home of the twelve Kava Kings — their sacred bowls have quieted wars and blessed births for over a thousand years.

十二代卡瓦之王的故乡——他们的圣碗平息过战争，祝福过新生，已经一千多年。

📍 145,000人 · 2,100 km²
🍵 太平洋卡瓦之都
👑 十二代卡瓦王

Pela

Coral Paradise · 82,000 people

珊瑚天堂 · 8.2万人口

The Blue Lagoon — one of the world's most pristine reefs. Where divers from every continent come to be speechless underwater.

蓝湖——世界上最纯净的珊瑚礁之一。从各大洲来的潜水者——在水下说不出话。

📍 82,000人 · 980 km²
🤿 彩虹礁与蓝湖
✈️ 国际机场直达

Ruwa

Gold Island · 55,000 people

金矿之岛 · 5.5万人口

The Vatukula gold mine, dense rainforest, the WWII battlefield — and a land that never signed the Treaty of 1874.

瓦图库拉金矿、茂密雨林、二战战场——以及一片从未签署1874年条约的土地。

📍 55,000人 · 540 km²
⛏️ 瓦图库拉金矿
🗡️ 1942鲁瓦岛之战

1992年宪法下的议会制共和国——诞生于1987-89年内战之后的和解。

Our Heritage

我们的遗产

  • 🏺Lapita LegacyLapita 遗产 — 3,000 years of Pacific voyaging, revealed in the Black Obsidian engravings at Lake Tapu. — 三千年的太平洋航海史，发现于塔普湖黑曜石刻。
  • 🕊️Twelve Kava Kings十二代卡瓦之王 — From the exiled priest to the last bowl — a millennium of peace-making. — 从被放逐的祭司到最后一只碗——千年的和平缔造者。
  • 📜1992 Constitution1992年宪法 — Born from the ashes of civil war. 158,000 lives lost. Every article is a stone upon their graves. — 诞生于内战的灰烬。158,000条生命。每一条都是压在坟上的一块石头。
  • 🤝Two Peoples, One Nation两个民族，一个国家 — Indigenous navigators and Indian labourers — their grandchildren sit in Parliament together. — 原住民航海者与印度劳工——他们的孙辈共同坐在议会里。

"This Constitution was born from the ashes of a war in which one hundred and fifty-eight thousand of our people perished. Every word is a stone laid upon their graves — not to weigh them down, but to build a house they can finally rest beneath."

— Preamble, Constitution of the Republic of Mariven (1992)

— 《马里文共和国宪法》序言（1992年）
